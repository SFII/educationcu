What is the SCQ project?

  • The SCQ project is an attempt to improve the current system of collecting and visualizing anonymous student survey data. We want to take seemingly dry and boring evaluation stats maintained and collected by universities, and present that data in a meaningful and attractive way. Collected data is made instantly accessible. Tools to visualize and study the data are available to everyone. We believe with this approach, clear actionable information can be made readily available to students, instructors and administrators.
